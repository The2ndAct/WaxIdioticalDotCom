Welcome to WaxIdiotical — a home for short films, 48 Hour Film Projects, theater montages, and whatever else ends up in front of a camera.

## What You'll Find Here

This site collects film work across a few categories:

- **48 Hour Films** — short films made under the pressure of a competition weekend
- **Theater Montages** — highlights from theatrical productions
- **Other** — everything that doesn't fit neatly elsewhere

Each film has its own page with an embed and some notes on how it came together.
