We are thrilled and deeply honored to share that *Silent Treatment* received **7 nominations** at the Boston 48 Hour Film Project — and took home **Best Acting Ensemble**.

## The Nominations

- **Best Picture**
- **Best Cinematography**
- **Best Acting (Individual Performance) — Annie Cicero**
- **Best Acting (Individual Performance) — Kyp Pilalas**
- **Best Use of Line**
- **Best Editing**
- **Best Titles**

## Best Acting Ensemble

To be recognized for ensemble performance means everything to us. Every person on set brought something essential to *Silent Treatment*, and this award belongs to all of them. Thank you to Annie Cicero, Fabulous Tustin, and Kyp Pilalas for their work in front of the camera, and to the entire crew who made it possible.

## Congratulations to Jump 667

A huge congratulations to **Jump 667**, winners of Best Picture. We had a great time competing alongside you and everyone else in this year's festival. The quality of filmmaking in this competition never stops being inspiring.

Thank you to the Boston 48 Hour Film Project for putting it all together, and to everyone who came out to watch.

Here's to the next one.
