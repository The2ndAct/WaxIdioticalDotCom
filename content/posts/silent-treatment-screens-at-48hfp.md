Last Wednesday night at 9:00pm, our latest 48 Hour Film, *Silent Treatment*, had its screening at the 48 Hour Film Project in Group D alongside a wonderful group of other films.

It was a great night. The theater was full of energy, and seeing the film play with an audience for the first time was everything we make these things for.

Now we wait. Audience award voting results are expected to be released this coming week. We're looking forward to seeing how the night shook out. Stay tuned.
